faq

q: why?
a: it must be done.

q: ok, seriously, what is this?
a: quick response (qr) codes are similar to bar codes. they are simply a different way of encoding text data in an image format easily scanned by a wide ran ge of special purpose devices and modern phones equipped with cameras and decoding software. most platforms have free decoding software available.

q: the decoding software is free!? what's this site all about then?
a: indeed. this site is about making qr technology fun, easy and profitable to use! we will link data embedded in custom qr codes with your friends on various social media sites, your customers, and anything else you can imagine!

q: how do i use these things and why would i want to?
a: simply download a barcode/qr code scanner from your smart phone app market of choice. there are several good scanners out there. most of them begin scanning as soon as you fire up the app, so just point your phone at a qr code (like the ones on this site) and you've got it.
